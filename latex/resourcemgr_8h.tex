\subsection{Detailed Description}
File for the audio class. 

This will take care of all the loading and playing of sound and music files that are used for the program. These functions should not be explicitly called by the user. The \hyperlink{class_resource_mgr}{ResourceMgr} will be calling all of these functions. Essentially, it will manage all of the resources of the project.
